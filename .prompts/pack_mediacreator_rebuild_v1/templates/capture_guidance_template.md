
# Capture guidance template

Every capture screen should include:

- how many images to take
- distance guidance
- front / side / back examples
- face closeups
- hair line visibility guidance
- arms away from torso guidance
- no busy background
- no motion blur
- consistent lighting
- clothing guidance for body capture
- optional high-detail texture capture guidance

The app should render example reference boards in Blender and store them as assets.
